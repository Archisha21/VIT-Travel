# 🚗 VIT RideShare – Smart Carpooling Platform for Students

<br>

## <b>📖 Project Description</b>

VIT RideShare is a web-based platform designed to help students securely find and share rides for common travel needs such as airport transfers, commuting between college and home, or other destinations.

<br>

The platform ensures <b>safety and authenticity</b> by allowing access only to verified VIT students through institutional login credentials.

<br>

Students who own or have access to a vehicle (ride providers) can create ride listings by specifying details such as destination, departure time, and available seats. Other students (ride seekers) can browse these rides and request to join.

<br>

Ride providers can <b>approve or reject requests</b>. Once approved, the number of available seats is automatically reduced. When all seats are filled, the ride is removed from active listings to prevent overbooking.

---

## <b>🎯 Objective</b>

- Reduce travel costs for students  
- Promote efficient vehicle usage  
- Provide a secure and trusted ride-sharing environment

## <b>🔑 Features</b>

- 🔐 <b>Secure Authentication</b><br>
  Login restricted to verified VIT student accounts  

<br>

- 🚗 <b>Ride Creation</b><br>
  Users can create ride listings with destination, time, and available seats  

<br>

- 🔍 <b>Ride Search</b><br>
  Browse and filter rides based on location and timing  

<br>

- ✅ <b>Request & Approval System</b><br>
  Users request to join rides; providers approve or reject  

<br>

- 🔢 <b>Dynamic Seat Management</b><br>
  Seats decrease automatically when requests are approved  

<br>

- ❌ <b>Auto Removal of Full Rides</b><br>
  Ride listings are removed when all seats are filled  

<br>

- 📊 <b>Real-Time Updates</b><br>
  Ensures accurate ride availability
